## 📖 About The Project

DPDPA Guardian AI is an enterprise-grade Privacy Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platform designed to help organizations comply with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023**.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence, automation, and real-time monitoring to continuously discover personal data, manage consent, assess compliance risks, monitor third-party vendors, and respond to privacy incidents.

Designed for **Banks, FinTechs, NBFCs, Insurance Companies, Enterprises, and Government Organizations**, DPDPA Guardian AI transforms privacy compliance from a manual process into an intelligent and automated operation.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Organizations today struggle with:

- Lack of visibility into personal data assets
- Manual compliance monitoring
- Consent tracking challenges
- Third-party vendor risks
- Delayed breach detection
- Complex regulatory reporting
- Resource-intensive audits

These challenges increase the risk of regulatory penalties, operational inefficiencies, and reputational damage.

## 💡 Solution

DPDPA Guardian AI provides a centralized platform that enables organizations to:

- 🔍 Discover and classify personal data automatically
- ✅ Manage consent throughout its lifecycle
- ⚠️ Detect compliance risks proactively
- 🔥 Respond to privacy incidents in real time
- 🏢 Monitor vendor compliance continuously
- 📊 Generate audit-ready compliance reports
